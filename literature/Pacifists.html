Pacifists with Colors

published: Mar 19, 2020

I've seen him burning
under a great large birch tree.
The planks were lit,
but
the leaves were still wet with the mist of tomorrow.
The Aurora in the sky danced
with the grace of Thunder.
Corrosive looks
started glaring at him,
though he was still flamboyant.
He lit the torches
with the sacred fire
on his shoulders.
The torch ignites with teal colored flames.
And the flying leaves
started orbiting around them
enchanted with the breath of god.

OH!
How I wanted him to keep going.
Eclipse after eclipse
he moves
as his body ran stale.
The warmth
that he generated could give hope to a whole new generation.
But his emerald eyes,
the last grain of starch
washed out as his emotions gave out.
And he flinched.....
